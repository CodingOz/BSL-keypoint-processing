British Sign Language Video Survey

Thank You for Participating!

I'm making an app for recognising and teaching some basic British Sign Language as my Final Year Project, so need footage of (hopefully) around 70 people doing a few basic signs.

🔒 Your Privacy is Protected

Your videos never leave your device. Hand-keypoint coordinates are extracted directly in your browser using on-device AI, and only those coordinates are sent to the server. The original video is permanently discarded without being uploaded. Here is an example of what the keypoint data looks like:

The anonymised keypoint data will be used solely for BSL recognition research and will not be shared with third parties.

Recording Instructions

  • Video examples are provided for each sign
  • Please match the general camera angle of the examples
  • Keep all videos under 5 seconds long
  • Keypoint extraction starts automatically once you choose a file — you'll see a green tick when it's ready
